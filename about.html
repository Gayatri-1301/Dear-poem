About Dear Poem

A community where poetry lovers and creators come together to share, celebrate, and inspire through the power of words.

Our Mission

Founded in 2018, Dear Poem is dedicated to fostering a love for poetry by providing a platform for poets to share their work and for readers to discover new voices. We believe poetry has the power to connect hearts and spark meaningful conversations.

Whether you're a seasoned poet or just starting, our community welcomes all who find solace, joy, or inspiration in verse.
