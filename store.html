Whispers of the Heart

Sarah J. Bennett

Description

A collection of tender poems exploring love, loss, and the quiet moments in between. Sarah J. Bennett's debut collection has touched thousands of readers with its raw honesty and lyrical beauty. These poems navigate the complexities of human emotion with grace and precision.

Sample Poem

The heart speaks in whispers,
not shouts—
a rustle of leaves,
the softest touch of light
on morning skin.

Listen closely,
for what it cannot say aloud
it writes in the quiet spaces
between breaths.
